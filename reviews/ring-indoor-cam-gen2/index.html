Ring Indoor Cam (2nd Gen) Review

★★★★½4.5/5$59.99

Ring's best indoor cam. Color night vision, a physical privacy shutter, and seamless integration with all other Ring devices.

$59.99 — Check Price on Amazon →

Full Review

The Ring Indoor Cam 2nd Gen is our top overall pick for 2025. The headline feature is color night vision — while most cameras switch to grainy black-and-white after dark, this one delivers full-color footage using an ambient light sensor. You can actually tell what color shirt someone is wearing at 2am.

The physical privacy cover is a game-changer. Slide it over the lens and the camera is physically blocked — no software trust required. This alone makes it the best choice for bedrooms or private spaces where you want guaranteed off-time.

Video quality is crisp 1080p with a wide 143-degree field of view. Motion detection is customizable with zones, and the Ring app is one of the most polished in the business. If you already have a Ring doorbell or other Ring devices, this integrates perfectly into the same ecosystem.

The downsides: you're locked into Amazon's ecosystem, and Ring Protect ($3.99/mo) is basically required for video history. Without it, you only get live view. There's also no local storage option — everything goes through the cloud.
