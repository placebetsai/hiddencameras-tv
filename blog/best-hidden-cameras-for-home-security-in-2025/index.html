Best Hidden Cameras for Home Security in 2025

January 1, 2025 · 3 min read

Whether you want to monitor a front door, keep an eye on a babysitter, or add a discreet camera to your living room, the market in 2025 has excellent options at every price point. Here are the cameras worth buying.

Our Top Picks

1. Blink Mini 2 — Best Budget Pick

At under $35, the Blink Mini 2 punches well above its price. It shoots 1080p HD video, supports two-way audio, and integrates with Alexa. The form factor is genuinely small — about the size of a golf ball — making it easy to position discreetly on a shelf or desk.

2. Ring Indoor Cam (2nd Gen) — Best Overall

The Ring Indoor Cam is our top all-around pick. It records in 1080p HD with color night vision and includes a physical privacy cover — a feature that builds trust if guests or housemates need reassurance the camera can be shut off. Clips integrate seamlessly with the Ring app.

Buy Ring Indoor Cam on Amazon →

3. Wyze Cam v4 — Best Value

Wyze cameras have always offered strong specs for the price, and the v4 model continues that tradition with 2K QHD resolution, color night vision, and a built-in siren. Wyze also offers 14 days of free cloud storage with no subscription — rare in this category.

Buy Wyze Cam v4 on Amazon →

What to Look for in a Hidden Camera

  • Resolution: 1080p is the minimum worth buying in 2025. 2K or 4K gives you usable zoom in footage.
  • Night vision: Color night vision is worth paying for — black-and-white IR footage makes identifying people much harder.
  • Storage: Local SD card storage is more private than cloud. If you choose cloud, check what the subscription costs after the free trial.
  • App reliability: Read recent reviews. Some brands ship great hardware with terrible apps.

Do You Need a Subscription?

Most cameras offer free basic features (live view, motion alerts) without a subscription. Cloud recording and AI-powered detection (person vs. car vs. animal) typically require a paid plan. Wyze Cam Plus is $1.99/month per camera. Ring Protect is $4/month. Google Nest Aware starts at $6/month.

If you want to avoid subscriptions entirely, look at Eufy cameras, which store footage locally on a base station or SD card with no monthly fee.

Our Recommendation

For most people, the Blink Mini 2 is the right starting point — it costs less than $35, works immediately, and has no required subscription. If you want better night vision and a more polished app experience, step up to the Ring Indoor Cam at $59.
