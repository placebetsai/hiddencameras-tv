What to Look for in a Hidden Camera

  • Resolution: 1080p is the minimum worth buying in 2025. 2K or 4K gives you usable zoom in footage.
  • Night vision: Color night vision is worth paying for — black-and-white IR footage makes identifying people much harder.
  • Storage: Local SD card storage is more private than cloud. If you choose cloud, check what the subscription costs after the free trial.
  • App reliability: Read recent reviews. Some brands ship great hardware with terrible apps.

Do You Need a Subscription?

Most cameras offer free basic features (live view, motion alerts) without a subscription. Cloud recording and AI-powered detection (person vs. car vs. animal) typically require a paid plan. Wyze Cam Plus is $1.99/month per camera. Ring Protect is $4/month. Google Nest Aware starts at $6/month.

If you want to avoid subscriptions entirely, look at Eufy cameras, which store footage locally on a base station or SD card with no monthly fee.
